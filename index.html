TripWise

Know a trip's real profit before you accept it.

TripWise helps gig-economy drivers (Uber, Bolt, Deliveroo and more) decide in seconds whether a trip offer is worth taking.

📱 Coming soon to Google Play

What it does

While a ride or delivery offer is on your screen, you tap the TripWise button. TripWise reads the offer, estimates your costs and the tax you should set aside, and shows a clear ACCEPT, MAYBE or REJECT — with your real take-home and the profit per kilometre or mile. You always make the final call; TripWise never accepts or rejects for you.

How it works

  1. Tap the TripWise button when a trip offer appears.
  2. TripWise reads it and estimates costs, taxes and real profit.
  3. Get a clear ACCEPT, MAYBE or REJECT with the reasoning.

Why drivers use it

Your real take-home

After fuel and other costs, and after a tax/social reserve — not just the gross fare.

Loss vs. low profit

See whether a rejected trip actually loses money or is simply below your benchmark.

Earnings insights

Take-home, accept rate, best hours and days, and which platform pays best.

Private by design

Your data stays on your device. The offer is read only when you tap — nothing is uploaded.
